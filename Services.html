Our Services

At Roaming Paws Veterinary Clinic, we offer a wide range of services to ensure the health and well-being of your pets. Our experienced veterinarians and staff are dedicated to providing compassionate care for your furry friends. Whether it's routine check-ups, vaccinations, or emergency care, we are here to support you and your pets every step of the way.



Preventive Care

Our preventive care services include regular check-ups, vaccinations, parasite control, and nutritional counseling to keep your pets healthy and happy.



Diagnostic Services

We offer a variety of diagnostic services, including blood tests, X-rays, ultrasound, and more to accurately diagnose and treat your pets' health issues.



Surgical Services

Our surgical services range from routine spaying and neutering to more complex procedures, all performed with the utmost care and precision.



Emergency Care

We provide emergency care for pets in critical condition, ensuring they receive immediate attention and treatment when they need it most.



Grooming and Boarding

In addition to medical care, we also offer grooming and boarding services to keep your pets looking and feeling their best while you're away.



Behavioral Counseling

Our behavioral counseling services help address any behavioral issues your pets may have, ensuring they are happy and well-adjusted members of your family.



Dental Care

We provide comprehensive dental care services, including cleanings, extractions, and oral health assessments to maintain your pets' dental health.



Senior Pet Care

Our senior pet care services focus on the unique needs of older pets, providing specialized care to ensure they enjoy their golden years in comfort and health.



Specialty Services

We also offer specialty services such as dermatology, cardiology, and oncology to provide comprehensive care for pets with specific health concerns.



Telemedicine

Our telemedicine services allow you to consult with our veterinarians remotely, providing convenient access to care for your pets from the comfort of your home.



Nutrition Counseling

Our nutrition counseling services help you choose the best diet for your pets, ensuring they receive the proper nutrients for optimal health.
